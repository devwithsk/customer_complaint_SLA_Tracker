# 🎫 Customer Complaint & SLA Breach Tracker

## 📌 Project Overview

The **Customer Complaint & SLA Breach Tracker** is an Excel-based support operations solution designed to help customer service teams monitor ticket resolution timelines, track Service Level Agreement (SLA) compliance, and proactively identify tickets at risk of breaching response deadlines.

The tool automatically calculates ticket aging, flags approaching SLA deadlines, highlights breached tickets, and provides management-level insights into support performance across agents and complaint categories.

---

# 🚀 Business Problem

Customer support teams handle hundreds of complaints, service requests, and support tickets every day. Without a structured monitoring system, organizations often face:

* Missed SLA deadlines
* Delayed customer responses
* Poor customer satisfaction
* Lack of visibility into ticket status
* Difficulty measuring agent performance
* Increased operational inefficiencies

When SLA commitments are not monitored effectively, businesses risk losing customer trust and damaging their service reputation.

---

# 💡 Solution

To address these challenges, I developed a **Customer Complaint & SLA Breach Tracker** in Microsoft Excel.

The solution automatically:

✅ Tracks complaint tickets and response timelines

✅ Calculates ticket age and SLA status

✅ Flags tickets nearing SLA deadlines

✅ Identifies breached tickets instantly

✅ Summarizes SLA compliance metrics

✅ Provides agent-wise and category-wise performance analysis

This enables support managers to take proactive action before SLA breaches occur and improve overall service quality.
